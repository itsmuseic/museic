MUSEIC

About the Artist

I’m MUSEIC — a songwriter, vocalist and independent artist who has built her music from the ground up.

Music has never been a passing interest in my life. From the moment I was born to the breath I am taking now, it has been the language through which I express myself most truthfully and the path I have chosen to build my life around.

About My Sound

I have developed a thirteen-track album that brings the R&B and pop sounds of the late 2000s and early 2010s—an era many listeners still miss and long to hear again—into the present through my own musical identity. My intention is not to recreate that period through nostalgia, but to draw inspiration from its melodies and character, then reshape them through contemporary production and my own artistic perspective.

The album is rooted primarily in R&B and pop. It also carries elements of Bass Pop, driven by powerful, deep low-end and a more physical impact. Some tracks move into a space I personally call Vibe House. This part of my sound is not designed only to make the listener dance; it exists to draw them into a mood, a story and an atmosphere.

The album also incorporates elements of rap and hip-hop. Alongside its melodic and sensual songs are harder, more confrontational tracks with an unapologetic attitude. These influences allow the record to move freely between atmosphere, emotion, power and storytelling without losing its identity.

The Process

Everything you hear on this album was created independently, and I carried the entire album process myself. I also develop and write my own music video concepts and treatments, because to me, the visual world of a song is part of the music itself.

These recordings were not made in a traditional studio. They were developed at home with the equipment available to me. The vocals you are currently hearing were recorded on a phone. I share this openly—not as an excuse, but because it is part of the truth behind the project I am presenting.

The final mixing and mastering have not yet been completed, but these recordings already carry a clear identity, imagination, honesty and countless hours of work.

The Journey

Throughout my journey, I have faced difficult professional experiences. I have worked with international companies in İzmir and İstanbul, but after my creative work was used and shared without permission, I chose to continue on my own path. I learned Ableton and spent years developing music and music-video projects independently.

Those experiences did not teach me to pity myself. They taught me to protect my voice, understand the industry more deeply and build a career in which my work remains my own.

I am ready to travel, collaborate, perform, learn and take risks. I have no travel restrictions and am available for international recording sessions, performances and creative collaborations.

The Vision

This project is still growing. What you hear today is an independently produced beginning, not the final destination. A much larger story and vision—one that has not yet been fully told—has been waiting for years to break through.

The world waiting to revolve around these songs is finally ready to begin its own journey.

Now, what I need is to meet the right person: someone who once recognized that same vision within themselves, refused to turn away from their path, and whose light on stage I now admire after watching them succeed.

Someone who can help the light within me finally be seen as well.
